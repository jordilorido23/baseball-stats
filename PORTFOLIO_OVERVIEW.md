## ‚úÖ What's Been Added (Latest Updates)

### **High-Priority Practical Projects**

1. **Time-Series Forecasting** (`src/models/timeseries_forecaster.py`)
   - ARIMA, Exponential Smoothing, Prophet models
   - In-season WAR projections
   - Rest-of-season forecasting for playoff scenarios
   - Walk-forward validation
   - **Why it matters**: Every team needs in-season projections. Universally applicable.

2. **Player Similarity Engine** (`src/analysis/player_similarity.py`)
   - Cosine similarity, K-Nearest Neighbors
   - Find comparable players for scouting/trading
   - Historical comps at specific ages
   - Radar chart visualizations
   - **Why it matters**: "Find me AAA players like Yordan Alvarez" - super practical scouting tool.

3. **Ensemble Model Stacking** (`src/models/ensemble_models.py`)
   - Combines Random Forest + XGBoost + Logistic Regression
   - Stacking classifier with meta-learner
   - Weighted ensembles based on validation performance
   - Model calibration for probability accuracy
   - **Why it matters**: Industry-standard ML practice. Shows you understand model evaluation.

4. **Pitch Arsenal Clustering** (`src/analysis/pitch_clustering.py`)
   - K-means, hierarchical, DBSCAN clustering
   - Group pitchers by stuff profiles
   - "Which pitchers have similar arsenals to Gerrit Cole?"
   - Dendrogram visualizations
   - **Why it matters**: Practical scouting application. Unsupervised learning demonstration.
